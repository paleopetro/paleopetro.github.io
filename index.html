About

PALEOPETRO was originally a company with a primary focus on work geology laboratory especially for support oil and gas exploration activities earth with specialization in Integrate lab data with other studies executed for the convenience of the client. PALEOPETRO focuses on data integration paleontology, petrography with quality high analytical work.

But in line with developments company, PALEOPETRO in addition to working on Laboratory work, with experience working in geological research, such as studies Biostratigraphy, Sequence Stratigraphy, Regional and Petroleum System and oil and gas block studies for the purposes of determining prospects and leads, reserve calculation, POD, new venturing, Due Diligence, Farm-In/Farm-Out. Field trips and Workshops related to exploration oil and gas, geology laboratory held regularly.!
